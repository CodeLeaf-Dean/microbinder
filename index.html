microbinder.js

Welcome to microbinder. A small javascript to html binding module. Everything you need to do most binding tasks in under 8kb. (3k gzipped!)

What does microbinder actually do?

microbinder takes a javascript object and allows you to bind its properties to your DOM through the use of templates and the bind attribute.
